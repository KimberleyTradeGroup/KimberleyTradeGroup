Who We Are

An Australian Trade Facilitator. In the Heart of Mumbai.

Kimberley Trade Group is an independent export facilitation agency operating across the Australia–India trade corridor. With direct market presence in Mumbai and full Australian regulatory standing, we bridge two of the world's most complementary economies.

We act as a pure introducer — identifying Australian supply, matching it with Indian demand, and structuring every transaction with legal protection for all parties. We take no title to goods, hold no inventory, and commit no capital.

Our model is built on one principle: we earn only when you do.

Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know.

What does Kimberley Trade Group actually do?+
We are an independent trade facilitator. We identify Australian suppliers, introduce them to qualified Indian buyers, and manage the documentation and communication flow throughout the transaction. We take no title to goods and earn a 5% commission only upon successful transaction completion.
Do I need to pay anything upfront?+
No. Our fee is strictly success-based — 5% of the gross transaction value, payable only when a transaction completes. There is no retainer, no consulting fee, and no upfront cost for either the buyer or the seller.
How does ECTA benefit me as an Indian buyer?+
The AI-ECTA agreement eliminates or significantly reduces import duties on a wide range of Australian goods entering India. This means sourcing directly from Australia through us is already cheaper than sourcing via intermediary hubs like Dubai or Singapore — before any negotiation on price.
How is payment handled? Is it safe?+
All transactions are settled via Letter of Credit (LC) through regulated banking institutions in both Australia and India. This is the global standard for secure international trade — funds are verified and secured at the bank level before goods move. We do not use informal escrow platforms.
What happens if there is a dispute between buyer and seller?+
Our commercial agreements clearly define that disputes between buyer and seller are resolved directly between those parties. Kimberley Trade Group's liability is strictly capped to the commission received on that transaction. All agreements are governed by Western Australian commercial law, with ACICA arbitration as the resolution mechanism.
Can I approach a supplier you've introduced me to directly in future?+
All parties sign our Non-Circumvention Agreement prior to introduction. This protects both the relationship and our facilitation role for a period of 2 years. After that period, both parties are free to transact independently.
